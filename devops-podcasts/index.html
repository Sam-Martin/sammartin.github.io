The Ship Show

The Ship Show is a twice-monthly podcast, featuring discussion on everything from build engineering to devops to release management, plus interviews, new tools and techniques, and reviews.

Arrested DevOps

Arrested DevOps is a high-level, bi-weekly panel discussion of DevOps concepts. We give our listeners a tantalizing taste of the basic technologies and ideas of DevOps to entice them to try more.

The Food Fight Show

Food Fight is a bi-weekly podcast for the Chef community. We bring together the smartest people in the Chef community and the broader DevOps world to discuss the thorniest issues in system administration.

Ops All The Things

Chris Webber and Steve Murawski talk all things operations.

RunAs Radio

RunAs Radio is a weekly Internet Audio Talk Show for IT Professionals working with Microsoft products. The full range of IT topics is covered from a Microsoft-centric viewpoint.
